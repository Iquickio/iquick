Product / IQ Base

IQuick is a full-featured software solution for cryptocurrency payments, for sale of goods and electronic services automatization. The IQuick made for a wide merchant area and it allows you to integrate the cryptocurrency payment vehicle into credit services, insurance or escrow services, online services and trading platforms.

Our IQuick protocol solves the problem of inefficient and costly payment mechanisms in electronic currency. Using a new smart contract architecture and a new billing method in the blockchain cutting cost on payment processing fees and minimizes risks by removing intermediaries such as banks and VISA or MasterCard payment systems.

IQuick protocol runs on a reliable blockchain of smart contracts governing the transactions. You can use IQuick payment mechanism “as is”, change it or create a new one based on it, depending on your business needs. IQuick creates additional opportunities for business and third-party companies that can build their services using the protocol and fill up the IQuick ecosystem.
